Discover Your Shine

Berean Crosspointe International (BCI) Marikina is a fully recognized institution by the Department of Education, offering programs from Nursery to Grade 12. In its advocacy to promote 21st Century Learning, BCI Marikina distinguishes itself by using the tagline "BCI Inspires. BCI Nurtures." as its guiding principle in helping its students to become the best version of themselves as God wants them to be.

Berean Onsite

The Berean Onsite (BOS) program is an onsite, teacher-led program that follows the BCI Marikina Academic Calendar. Lessons are facilitated by teachers based on prepared study plans. This program is available for students from Nursery to Grade 12.

Berean Online

The Berean Online (BOL) program is an online, teacher-led program that follows the BCI Marikina Academic Calendar. Lessons are facilitated by teachers based on prepared study plans. This program is available for students from Grades 4 to 10.

Berean Homeschool

The Berean Homeschool (BHS) program is a home-based, parent-led program that follows the routine and schedule set by the Parent-Facilitator. Lessons are facilitated by the parents or a tutor under the parent's supervision. This program is available for students from Nursery to Grade 10.

Faith, Fire, & A Decade of Dreams: The Story of BCI Marikina

Ten years ago, BCI Marikina was just a vision—a dream fueled by faith and perseverance. Today, that dream has grown into something greater than anyone imagined.

On February 26, 2025, the BCI Marikina community received its biggest news yet—the move to a new campus. Shouts of joy erupted from the students as they had a glimpse into their new home: a five-storey building with a basketball court and a spacious yard, located only five minutes away from their current campus.
