PROGRAMS

Berean Online

The Berean Online (BOL) program is an online, teacher-led program that follows the BCI Marikina Academic Calendar. Lessons are facilitated by teachers based on prepared study plans. This program is available for students from Grades 4 to 10.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Grade Levels: Grades 4 - 10

Learning Delivery: Teacher-Led (Major Subjects), Parent-Led (Minor Subjects)

Learning Materials: Online Materials

Training: Orientation & Parenting Talks

Assessments: Teacher-Led Quizzes/Tests, Achievement Tests, Socio-Emotional Tests, Quarterly Exams (Onsite)

Community Engagement: Berean Events: Family Assembly, Family Day, Thanksgiving Celebration, Creative Arts & Athletics Department (CAAD) Classes, CSR Initiatives

Counselling: Berean High, Counselling Service
