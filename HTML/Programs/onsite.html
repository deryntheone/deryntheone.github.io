PROGRAMS

Berean Onsite

The Berean Onsite (BOS) program is an onsite, teacher-led program that follows the BCI Marikina Academic Calendar. Lessons are facilitated by teachers based on prepared study plans. This program is available for students from Nursery to Grade 12.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Grade Levels: Nursery - Grade 12

Learning Delivery: Teacher-Led (All Subjects)

Learning Materials: Printed Materials

Training: Orientation & Parenting Talks

Assessments: Teacher-Led Quizzes/Tests, Achievement Tests, Socio-Emotional Tests, Quarterly Exams

Community Engagement: Berean Events: Family Assembly, Family Day, Thanksgiving Celebration, Creative Arts & Athletics Department (CAAD) Classes, CSR Initiatives

Counselling: Guidance Hour, Berean High, Counselling Service
