Admissions

Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry Submission

When a person inquires about the admissions process, we provide an overview and request essential details, such as:

  • Name of Person Inquiring
  • Relationship to Student
  • Contact Number
  • Email
  • Name of Student
  • Incoming Grade Level
  • Previous School
  • City of Residence
  • Preferred Program (Onsite/Online/Homeschooling)
  • How They Heard About BCI Marikina

Once submitted, our team will promptly provide details about programs, tuition, and admissions procedures via email.

Step 2: Application Submission & Payment

Applicants must pay the P300 Application Fee, which can be settled onsite at our new location or online. Upon payment confirmation, we schedule students for the Entrance Examination (EE) or Family Interview (FI) (same-day scheduling).

Students applying to the Berean Homeschool (BHS) program do not need to take the Entrance Examination and will proceed directly to the Family Interview.

Step 3: Entrance Examination & Family Interview

Applicants are scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis. Confirmation emails are sent with details of the required materials and the exam and interview date.

Step 4: Admissions Result & Enrollment

Accepted students will receive an official Acceptance Letter and instructions on how to proceed with enrollment.

Enrollment Location & Schedule

  • Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Cash
  • Check
  • Major credit/debit cards
  • GCash

Required Documents

  • Original Report Card with LRN
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • For Preschool Students: Medical Certificate stating “Fit to go to school”
  • For Students with Special Needs: Latest report from Developmental Pediatrician & Therapy Reports
  • Proof of applicable discounts
  • Recommendation Forms (if applicable)

Additional Step: Parents must print, fill out, and submit the Student Gate Pass/Fetcher’s ID form for stamping and lamination (P10 fee applies).

Step 5: Final Preparations & Orientation

Once officially enrolled, the class adviser will provide updates on:

  • ID photo-taking
  • Textbook payment and release
  • Class schedules and section names

Some important dates to remember are the following:

  • July 4, 2025: Parent's Orientation
  • July 9, 2025: Tentative Opening for A.Y. 2025 - 2026

We highly encourage onsite enrollment to ensure a smooth process!

Payment Methods & Discounts

The school fees may be paid on an Annual, Semestral, Quarterly, or Monthly basis. Schedules of payment are determined at the beginning of the enrollment period. Fees may be paid in the cashier’s office via cash, cheque, credit/debit card. For off-site payments, online bank transfers, GCash, and bank deposits are also accepted. BCI Marikina gives discounts on deserving students. A list of the discounts per category are listed below:

Academic Discounts (only for Non Homeschool Students)

  • First Rank in Class or a General Average of 98% and above for a BCI student
  • Second Rank in Class or a General Average of 96% – 97.99% for a BCI student
  • Third Rank in Class or a General Average of 94% – 95.99% for a BCI student

Transferees from another school are required to submit a Certification Letter from the Principal of the previous school certifying that the said transferee has been a recipient of such rank.

Economic Discount

  • This benefit is given if more than 2 siblings are enrolled in the school during the same school year. The school gives a discount beginning from the 3rd child onwards (4th, 5th etc.).
  • Single parents may also apply for this type of discount for one of their legitimate children. (Please present a Solo Parent ID)
  • Pursuant to Republic Act 10754, discounts are also given to students bearing a PWD ID. 

Ministerial Discount

  • The school also gives discounts for Children of Full-Time Pastors

A letter from the church council or board must be presented, certifying that the parent of the child is a full-time pastor.

Partnership Discount

Discounts are given to the following:

  • Community Bible Church of Marikina Members
  • Children of School Faculty and Staff
  • Close relatives of School Faculty and Staff (subject to prior management approval)
  • Children of Marikina City Government Employees

Public School Assistance Discount

  • BCI Marikina also gives Public School Transferees a discount during their first year in the school.

Student Athlete Discount

BCI Marikina celebrates and honors achievements in the area of sports and gives discounts to students who have received medals in the sports which they have represented the school. This discount is given during the academic year the award is achieved.

In cases when the student is qualified for more than ONE (1) category, discount that which gives the highest benefit from any TWO (2) categories can be availed.

President's Scholarship

A full 100% Tuition fee scholarship grant is awarded to a BCI Marikina student who has achieved the Highest Honors in the Grade Level Six (6) level as an incoming Grade Seven (7) student, as well as the student who finished Grade Ten (10) with Highest Honors as an incoming Grade Eleven (11) student.  In order to maintain this scholarship in the succeeding years, the student should achieve all of the following:

  1. Retain the rank of First Honors in class.
  2. Maintain a grade of 90% or better in all academic subjects.
  3. Maintain a Conduct grade of E (Excellent) or equivalent to 95% and above.
  4. No disciplinary action imposed at any time during the academic year.

DepEd Senior High School Voucher System

This voucher program is intended for Grade 10 Completers who wish to pursue Senior High School education. It enables students to claim discounts or deduction from tuition fees charged by an SHS institution where he/she enrolls. Since BCI Marikina is a private school, BCI Grade 10 completers may individually apply for the voucher.
