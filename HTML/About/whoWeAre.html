Who We Are

Berean Crosspointe International (BCI) Marikina is a Christian school that bridges the Traditional-Conventional approach to the Progressive type of the Teaching-Learning Method. BCI Marikina puts emphasis on the Experiential or Hands-on Learning with the goal of developing student capacity for Self-Directed Learning, Teamwork, Goal Orientation, Sense of Responsibility, Accountability, and above all, Godly Christian Character.

Established in 2015, BCI Marikina is a recognized school by the Department of Education (DepEd) that caters to students from Pre-School to Senior High School levels. BCI Marikina's curriculum integrates various 21st Century Learning Skills and International Programs offered for the Onsite, Online, and Homeschool Programs approved by DepEd under the Learning Continuity Plan (LCP).
​Located at #293 General Ordoñez St. in Barangay Marikina Heights, BCI Marikina is owned and headed by Rev. Raul M. Caguin (School President) and Mrs. Concepcion C. Caguin (School Director). Moreover, the school is operated by a competent Academic Team of Christians who are actively serving in the ministries of their respective churches.
